Goals of the Max/IRCAM Tri-Cities User Group

The aim of the User Group is to help Live users network and share information and experience in an informal environment with each other. The meetings are to allow all participants to suggest discussion themes related, Max for Live as well as to provide a forum for presenting their own work.
